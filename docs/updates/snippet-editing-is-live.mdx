# Snippet editing is live!
> Mon Mar 27 2023 14:30:34 GMT+0000 (Coordinated Universal Time)
<p>Throughout the last few weeks, we’ve received a ton of feedback and ideas from users and we couldn’t be more appreciative. We’re listening intently and working hard to make Pieces indispensable to your workflow.<p.new.line><p>One of the most common requests (especially from our internal users!) is to be able to lightly edit a snippet saved in Pieces. <strong>Well, we’re happy to let you know that now you can!</strong><p.new.line><h2 id="2nskt">Pieces for Developers<h2.new.line><p><em>Get started for free <a href="http://code.pieces.app/install" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">here</a>.
