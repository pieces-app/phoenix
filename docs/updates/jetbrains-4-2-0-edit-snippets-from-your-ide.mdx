# JetBrains 4.2.0: Edit snippets from your IDE
> Mon Mar 27 2023 15:27:48 GMT+0000 (Coordinated Universal Time)
<p>With today’s release, we’re bringing one of our most-requested features directly into JetBrains: Snippet editing.
    Now, you can make adjustments to your snippets without opening the Pieces desktop app.</p><p>To edit a snippet in
    the JetBrains suite:</p><ol type="1">
    <li>Right-click in the code</li>
    <li>Select a snippet from the Pieces menu</li>
    <li>Select “View/Edit”</li>
    <li>Edit your snippet</li>
    <li>Hit <code>ctrl+S</code> or <code>⌘+S</code> to save your edits</li>
