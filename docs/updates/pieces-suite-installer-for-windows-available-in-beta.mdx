# Pieces Suite Installer for Windows available in beta!
> Mon Mar 27 2023 14:20:30 GMT+0000 (Coordinated Universal Time)

At Pieces, we’re obsessed with improving processes to make them faster for our users. That’s why we are excited to now offer a quicker, more efficient way to install the Pieces app on Windows.

<strong>Introducing the Pieces Suite Installer </strong>- the all-in-one package application that downloads and installs Pieces OS and Pieces for Developers in a single click!

![](https://uploads-ssl.webflow.com/6143afec68f555387049efb3/630d6cdadbab7a7569f9fa8a_ezgif_com-gif-maker13_a3f5da143a6a48652be7c00623cdd03f_800.png)

In addition to a more seamless onboarding experience, the Pieces Suite Installer offers users the ability to:
- Explore and download Pieces integrations for your IDE and browser
- Check for and manually push updates for Pieces OS and PFD

## Getting Started with Pieces Suite Installer
The Pieces Suite Installer is available in beta on our website, get it <a href="https://code.pieces.app/install" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">here</a>.*

***If you are already a Pieces user with downloads from the Microsoft store, you must uninstall older versions of Pieces to align with this new system.**
